Venue

TNSAA 2026 will be held at:

RIKEN Center for Computational Science (R-CCS)#

Kobe, Japan

The RIKEN Center for Computational Science (R-CCS) is one of the world’s leading research centers for high-performance computing and computational science. The workshop venue is located in Kobe, a major international port city in western Japan.

Access#

From Kansai International Airport (KIX)#

  • Airport Limousine Bus to Sannomiya Station
  • JR train connections available
  • Approx. 70–90 minutes

From Shin-Kobe Station#

  • Subway + Port Liner access
  • Approx. 30 minutes

Local Transportation#

Detailed transportation information will be provided closer to the workshop dates.

Accommodation#

Participants are recommended to stay near:

  • Sannomiya
  • Motomachi
  • Kobe Harborland

Hotel information and recommended options will be announced later.

Visa Information#

Participants requiring a visa to enter Japan are encouraged to contact the organizers in advance.

Additional information and invitation letter procedures will be announced later.

About Kobe#

Kobe is an international port city known for its waterfront, modern architecture, and excellent accessibility. The city offers convenient transportation, international cuisine, and cultural attractions.
